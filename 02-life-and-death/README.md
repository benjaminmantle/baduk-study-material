# 02 · Life & Death (Tsumego)

**Reading is the #1 strength skill at every level.** Do a little every day — this folder is the
engine room of the whole collection.

**Subfolders**
- **[`graded/`](graded/)** — problems sorted by difficulty. *Start here* and build the daily habit.
- **[`special-shapes/`](special-shapes/)** — the shapes that decide real games: L-group, J-group,
  carpenter's square, comb, bent-four, big eyes, and life & death straight **out of joseki**.
- **[`collections/`](collections/)** — the classic problem books (Gokyo Shumyo, Xuanxuan Qijing,
  Guanzi Pu, Cho Chikun, Segoe) plus graded modern sets.

Also here: `sdk-essential-life-and-death.sgf` (SDK).

**How to use:** 20–30 problems a day, easy enough that you go *fast* — pattern speed matters more
than grinding one hard problem. Re-solve whole sets until the answers are instant.
