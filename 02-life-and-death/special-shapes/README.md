# Special shapes — the life & death that decides real games

The highest-leverage, most *practical* life & death: recurring corner/side shapes and the status
questions that come straight out of joseki. Know these cold and you'll win games you used to lose.

**The canon to master (SGF references being collected):**
- **L-group** — the classic 6-space corner group; know the alive / dead / ko cases.
- **J-group** — its bulkier cousin; common kills and vital points.
- **Carpenter's square** — famous corner shape with many variations, several involving ko.
- **Comb formation** — long side-group life & death.
- **Bent four in the corner** — the classic (plus its interesting rules history).
- **Big-eye / nakade shapes** — straight three, bent three, pyramid four, bulky five, rabbity six:
  which are alive, which die, and the killing placement.
- **From-joseki corners** — the life & death that arises from common joseki, e.g. the 3-3 invasion
  under the 4-4 stone, and standard corner josekis where the status is the whole point.
