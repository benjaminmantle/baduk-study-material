# 08 · Endgame / Yose

The most **underrated** points on the board. Move values, sente vs. gote, double-sente, *tedomari*
(getting the last big point), and counting. Even a rough sense of yose flips games you thought you
were losing — most amateurs leave 10+ points on the table here every game.

**Here:** [`booklets-pdf/`](booklets-pdf/) — endgame technique books (Go Seigen, Sakata, Nie
Weiping, Lee Chang-ho, New Korean Endgame Dictionary, and more).

Rule of thumb to start: play double-sente first, then big sente, then big gote; a reverse-sente
move is worth roughly double its face value.
