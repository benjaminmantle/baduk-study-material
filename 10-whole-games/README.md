# 10 · Whole Games

Professional and historical game records. **Play them through — quickly** — to absorb shape,
direction, and "what good looks like." One game a day played fast beats one game a month
over-analyzed.

**Subfolders**
- **[`classic/`](classic/)** — the old masters: Shusaku, Dosaku, Jowa, Go Seigen, Shusai…
- **[`modern/`](modern/)** — contemporary pro games (title matches, international, Fox-server pros).
- **[`ai-era/`](ai-era/)** — AlphaGo & successors; the games that rewrote theory.
- **[`by-player/`](by-player/)** — single-player career collections.
- **[`by-tournament/`](by-tournament/)** — Kisei, Meijin, Honinbo, and other titles.
- **[`collections/`](collections/)** — large mixed packs (e.g. the badukmovies pro collection).

Want *everything* at once? See the bulk-dataset links in [provenance](../docs/provenance.md).
