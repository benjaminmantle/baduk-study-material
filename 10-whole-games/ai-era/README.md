# AI-era games

The games that reshaped how Go is played.

- **[`alphago-selfplay-50/`](alphago-selfplay-50/)** — the 50 AlphaGo-vs-AlphaGo self-play games
  DeepMind released in 2017. ⭐
- **[`alphago-master-series/`](alphago-master-series/)** — AlphaGo's 60 online games as
  "Master"/"Magist" vs. the world's top pros (Dec 2016–Jan 2017). ⭐

**What AI changed to look for:** immediate 3-3 invasions under the 4-4, early shoulder hits,
fewer slow/territorial moves, and a willingness to take thickness lightly. Study the *why*.
