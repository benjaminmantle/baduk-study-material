# Games by title & tournament

Compressed SGF packs of the major Japanese titles and fast-game tournaments. **Public domain**;
extract with `tar -xzf name.tgz`.

- **The big three:** `kisei.tgz`, `meijin.tgz`, `honinbo.tgz` ⭐
- **Other titles:** `judan.tgz`, `oza.tgz`, `tengen.tgz`, `gosei.tgz`
- **International:** `ing.tgz` (Ing Cup)
- **Fast games:** `nhk.tgz` (NHK Cup), `ryusei.tgz`

Source: the CWI/Brouwer public archive.
