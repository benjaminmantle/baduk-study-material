# 12 · Proverbs & Principles

Condensed, memorable wisdom — training wheels that genuinely work while your reading catches up.

A starter set:
- Corners → sides → center.
- Don't attach to weak stones (it strengthens them).
- Play away from thickness — yours *and* your opponent's.
- The rich man shouldn't pick quarrels.
- Answer a hane at the head of two stones.
- If you don't know joseki, lose two stones' worth; if you know too much, lose two stones' worth.
- When in doubt, tenuki.
