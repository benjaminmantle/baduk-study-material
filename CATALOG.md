### Quick "what should I download first?"
- **Get stronger fastest:** [GoGameGuru graded problems](02-life-and-death/graded/gogameguru-weekly/) + do them daily.
- **Fix your openings:** play through [Shusaku](10-whole-games/classic/) and the [AI-era games](10-whole-games/ai-era/).
- **Joseki without pain:** [1-dan essentials](05-joseki/) then dip into [Kogo's](05-joseki/kogos-joseki-dictionary.sgf).
- **Shape sense:** read [Shape Up!](06-shape/shape-up_matthews-kim.pdf) once, cover to cover.
