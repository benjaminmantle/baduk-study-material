# License, Copyright & Takedown

This repository is a **curated, non-commercial, educational collection** of freely available
material for studying the game of Go (Baduk / Weiqi). It mixes several categories with different
rights, so please read this.

## Original content in this repo → public domain (CC0)
All material **written for this repository** — the README, folder guides, `docs/` (study paths,
glossary, catalog, provenance), naming, and organization — is released under
[**CC0 1.0**](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/): do whatever you like with it,
no attribution needed.

## Game records (SGF) → facts, freely shared
The **moves of a Go game are facts**, not a creative work, and game records circulate freely
throughout the worldwide Go community (as chess games do). SGF files here are provided on that
basis. Where a *commentary* adds original authored content, that commentary belongs to its author.

## Historical problem collections → positions are old, editions vary
Classic problem books (Gokyo Shumyo, Xuanxuan Qijing / Gengen Gokyo, Guanzi Pu, etc.) are
centuries old and their **positions are public domain**. Specific modern scans, typesetting, or
translations may carry their own rights. These are flagged **⚠** in
[`docs/provenance.md`](docs/provenance.md).

## Third-party books & PDFs → original rights retained
Any full-length book or PDF here is included only where believed to be **public-domain, freely
released by its author/publisher, or a long-circulated community file**. Copyright in those works
remains with their owners. We do **not** host scans of in-print commercial books — those are
linked for purchase in [`docs/recommended-reading.md`](docs/recommended-reading.md).

## Takedown — fast and no-questions
If you hold rights to something in this repository and want it removed, **open an issue** (or
contact the owner) and it will be taken down promptly. The goal here is to help people learn Go,
never to harm authors or publishers — the Go book world is small and we want it to thrive.
