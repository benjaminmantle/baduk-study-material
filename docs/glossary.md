# Cross-Language Go Terminology

Go is played worldwide and most English terms are borrowed from Japanese. This table maps the
core vocabulary across **English · Japanese · Korean · Chinese** so you can navigate material —
and search sites — in any of the four languages.

> Format: **English** — 日本語 *(rōmaji)* — 한국어 *(rom.)* — 中文 *(pīnyīn)*
> Corrections welcome via issue/PR. Korean/Chinese romanizations are approximate.

| Move / a play | 手 *te* | 수 *su* | 手 *shǒu* |

### Handy search terms by language
When mining non-English sites, these keywords surface the good stuff:
- **Chinese:** 围棋 死活 手筋 定式 棋谱 官子 布局 让子 (weiqi · life-death · tesuji · joseki · game records · endgame · opening · handicap)
- **Korean:** 바둑 사활 맥 정석 기보 끝내기 포석 접바둑
- **Japanese:** 囲碁 詰碁 手筋 定石 棋譜 ヨセ 布石 置碁
